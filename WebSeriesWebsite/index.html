Welcome to the world of La Casa De Papel

Money Heist a.k.a La Casa De Papel is a spanish web show that resembles the story of resistance of a gang who have some dreams. It is a story of two genius brothers that assemble a gang and plan to burgle the Royal Mint of Spain. For simplification they name the team members as city names. The heist is filled with alot of twists and turns and the gang itself falls into pieces at times but the moto of resistance is able to keep them intact and complete the heist which creates history with the public support on there end.
